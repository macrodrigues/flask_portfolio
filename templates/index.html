About

With over 3 years of engineering experience, I can tag myself as a builder. With a Masters in Nanotechnology and Microelectronics, it took me a few years to understand that the easiest and fastest way to build is with a simple laptop. Today, I'm mostly a Software Engineer highly focused on Python and AI. The change was progressive, early on, I was learning Python after my working hours, the pandemic kinda of helped me to have more time to dive into it. Later on, I got a job where I mixed microelectronics and programming. And finally, I decided to learn more about this new world of Machine Learning and Deep Learning, so I enrolled in a Data Science bootcamp. A delightful experience that gave me the key skills to pursue a career as a developer. Not long after, I got a job in French Guiana. I packed up the essentials, and flew to the Amazon rainforest to work as a Software Engineer at the European Spaceport. Nature gave me the breeze I needed to keep building, and being creative.
